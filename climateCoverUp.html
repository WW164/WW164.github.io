Climate Cover-up

Climate Cover-up is a serious game made as part of my master's degree in my Serious Gaming course. This serious goal of this game is to raise awareness about fake news regarding climate change. It put players in a leader's shoe, which their goal is to destroy other countries while covering up the truth about climate change. In this project, I was responsible for:

  • Designing the economy
  • Implementing sound effects
