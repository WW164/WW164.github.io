Potion Master

Description

Potion Master 3D is a simulation hypercasual game in which players can brew potions and see its effect on the customers. This game successfully passed the CPI test and currently I'm working on its 3rd iteration on Retention phase. In this project, I was responsible for:

Responsibilities

  • Designing core loop and gameplay mechanics
  • Writing 20+ scenarios for levels
  • Designing 20+ levels
  • Writing 5+ ad scenarios
  • Play testing and bug report
  • Designing in-game economy including in-game shop, minigames, rewards, etc
  • Designing monetization strategies such as rewarded videos, interstitial ads. and etc
