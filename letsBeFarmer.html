Let's be Farmer

Let's be Farmer is a roleplay hypercasual game in which players take the role of a farmer who sell his/her goods to customers. I worked on this project at Going Hyper 2021 and won the Best Game Design and 3rd Best Game award. In this project, I was responsible for:

  • Designing the core loop
