Musical Attention Control Training

Musical Attention Control Training is a dancing serious game in which players take control of a pinguin. There are three levels in total where a song is played with musical cues and players have to respond to the musical cues. Based on how fast or slow they respond to the musical cue their displayed feedback changes. This project aims to improve player's attention. I worked on this project as a part of Sound and Music Technology course at Utrecht University. In this project, I was responsible for:

  • Designing the UI in the main scene
  • Implementing songs and musical cues
  • Designing levels
